Brother.

Brother. is an indie rock band based in Provo, UT. We've been a band since Mid 2016. I joined as the drummer, but at the end of 2018 switched to guitar and bass. Feel free to check out official website here.

Events

Rooftop

During the Summer of 2018, we were asked to play the Rooftop Concert Series with The National Parks and The Solarists. There were around 3000 people in the audience. It's still one of our favorite shows we've ever played.

Europe

In early 2019, we had the opportinity to go to Europe as a band. We traveled to Zurich, Switzerland; Dublin, Irland; London, England; and Paris, France. We had a blast and were able to play a local show in London at Canverdish Arms.
